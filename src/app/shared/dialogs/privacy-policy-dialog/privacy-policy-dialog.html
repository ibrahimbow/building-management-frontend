Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

1. Introduction

JORITNA (“the Platform”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the integrity of any information processed through our application.

This Privacy Policy outlines how limited data is collected, utilized, and managed while interacting with the JORITNA platform.

2. Demo / Testing Environment

JORITNA is currently operating as a demo and testing platform intended strictly for development, educational, portfolio, and evaluation purposes.

Users are explicitly advised NOT to upload or share authentic, sensitive personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Real account passwords
  • Financial or banking information
  • Government identification numbers (IDs, passports)
  • Medical or health-related information
  • Confidential personal or corporate documents

To preserve privacy within this testing environment, users should exclusively utilize fictional, placeholder, or non-sensitive data.

Furthermore, please be aware that all user profiles, system logs, and data submissions are subject to periodic, permanent deletion without prior notice to facilitate continuous testing and development.

3. Information Collected

The Platform may collect limited information voluntarily submitted by users to facilitate core functionality, including:

  • Display names and user aliases
  • Email addresses (for authentication purposes)
  • Optional profile images
  • Building membership configurations
  • Manager announcements, tenant messages, and community posts
  • Voluntarily uploaded images or files

The architecture of the Platform is strictly engineered to minimize unnecessary data collection and exposure.

4. Use of Information

Any data processed by the Platform is used solely for:

  • User authentication and secure account access
  • Delivering real-time application and feature functionality
  • Displaying localized community content and messaging
  • Analyzing platform stability, monitoring bugs, and optimization
  • Database migrations and backend service verification

5. Data Visibility

  • Only display names and optional profile pictures are visible to other verified members within your designated testing environment.
  • Email addresses and private communication channels are intended to remain hidden from general users unless explicitly shared or broadcasted by the user.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and operational safeguards are implemented to protect the Platform’s data integrity, including:

  • JWT-based (JSON Web Token) stateless authentication
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) mechanisms
  • Secured microservice communications
  • Isolated, containerized cloud infrastructure

However, because this is an active development environment, no system can guarantee absolute security. Users assume full responsibility for the data they choose to submit.

7. Third-Party Services

JORITNA utilizes trusted third-party infrastructure and open-source technologies to power its deployment, container routing, and messaging pipelines:

  • Cloudflare
  • Docker
  • GitHub
  • Hetzner
  • PostgreSQL
  • Apache Kafka

8. Limitation of Liability

As a non-commercial portfolio and testing environment, JORITNA and its developers shall not be held liable for:

  • The loss, corruption, or automatic purging of testing data
  • The exposure of any real-world sensitive information submitted in violation of Section 2
  • The misuse or exploitation of shared demo accounts
  • Temporary server outages, latency, or unexpected downtime

9. Future Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated or modified periodically to reflect architectural changes or new features. Continued use of the Platform following an update constitutes acceptance of the modified policy terms.

10. Contact Information

For questions, feedback, or technical inquiries regarding data processing on JORITNA, please reach out through the project's official repository channels.
